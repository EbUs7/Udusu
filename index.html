Learn More About Staking

What is Staking? Staking is a way to earn rewards for holding certain cryptocurrencies. By "staking" your crypto, you lock it up to help maintain the security and operations of a blockchain network. In return, you earn more cryptocurrency.

How it Works: When you stake your TON with Shuaa Capital, your funds are added to our secure staking pool. We manage the staking process, and you receive a share of the rewards generated by the network, minus a small fee for our services.

Benefits: Earn passive income, contribute to network security, and maintain liquidity (you can unstake your funds at any time).
